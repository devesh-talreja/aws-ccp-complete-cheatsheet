## SUPPORT PLANS

### Basic (Free)
- AWS Trusted Advisor: 7 core checks
- 24/7 customer service
- Documentation, whitepapers, forums

### Developer ($29/month)
- Business hours email support
- General guidance < 24 hours
- System impaired < 12 hours

### Business ($100/month minimum)
- 24/7 phone, email, chat
- Production system impaired < 4 hours
- Production system down < 1 hour
- Trusted Advisor: ALL checks
- Use case guidance

### Enterprise On-Ramp ($5,500/month minimum)
- Business-critical system down < 30 minutes
- Concierge Support Team
- Infrastructure Event Management (one per year)
- Access to pool of Technical Account Managers (TAMs)

### Enterprise ($15,000/month minimum)
- Business-critical system down < 15 minutes
- Designated Technical Account Manager (TAM)
- Infrastructure Event Management
- Unlimited

**Memory Trick:** "Basic < Developer < Business < Enterprise" (more expensive = faster response)
