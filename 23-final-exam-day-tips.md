## FINAL EXAM DAY TIPS

### Before the Exam:
1. ✅ Review this cheat sheet 2-3 times
2. ✅ Take practice exams (aim for 80%+ consistently)
3. ✅ Focus on weak areas identified in practice
4. ✅ Get good sleep (not cramming night before)
5. ✅ Eat a good meal before exam

### During the Exam:
1. **Read questions carefully** - keyword is often in the question
2. **Flag difficult questions** - come back later
3. **Eliminate wrong answers first** - process of elimination
4. **Watch for "MOST" and "LEAST"** - comparative questions
5. **Time management:** ~1.5 minutes per question (90 min for 65 questions)
6. **Don't overthink** - first instinct is often correct
7. **Review flagged questions** - if time permits

### Common Question Patterns:
- "Which service should be used to...?" → Know service purposes
- "What is the MOST cost-effective solution?" → Compare pricing models
- "How can the company achieve high availability?" → Multi-AZ, regions
- "Which is a responsibility of the customer?" → Shared Responsibility Model
- "A company needs to comply with...?" → Security and compliance services

### Red Flags in Answers (Usually Wrong):
- ❌ "Use IAM User access keys" (should use IAM Roles)
- ❌ "Store credentials in code" (use Secrets Manager)
- ❌ "Single AZ deployment" (when HA is needed)
- ❌ "Manual scaling" (when Auto Scaling available)
- ❌ "Public subnet for databases" (should be private)

### Green Flags in Answers (Usually Right):
- ✅ "Use IAM Roles"
- ✅ "Multi-AZ deployment"
- ✅ "Enable encryption"
- ✅ "Use CloudWatch for monitoring"
- ✅ "Implement least privilege"
- ✅ "Use managed services"
