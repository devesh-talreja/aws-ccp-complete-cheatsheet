## PRICING QUICK REFERENCE

### Free Tier (12 months after signup):
- **EC2:** 750 hours/month of t2.micro or t3.micro
- **S3:** 5GB standard storage
- **RDS:** 750 hours/month of db.t2.micro
- **Lambda:** 1 million requests/month (always free)
- **DynamoDB:** 25GB storage (always free)
- **CloudFront:** 50GB data transfer out (always free)

### Pay-As-You-Go Services:
- EC2 (per second/hour)
- Lambda (per request + duration)
- S3 (per GB stored + requests)
- RDS (per hour)
- Data Transfer OUT (data in is usually free)

### Data Transfer Pricing:
- **IN to AWS:** Generally FREE
- **OUT from AWS:** Charged (varies by region)
- **Between regions:** Charged
- **Within same region (private IP):** FREE
- **Using CloudFront:** Can reduce costs

**Memory Trick:** "Data IN is free, OUT costs money - AWS wants your data!"
