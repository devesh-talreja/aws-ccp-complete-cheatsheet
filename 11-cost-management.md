## 11. COST MANAGEMENT

### Cost Explorer
**What:** Visualize and analyze costs
**Real-life:** Like checking your bank statements with charts
**Key Points:**
- Historical data
- Forecasting
**Memory Trick:** "Cost Explorer = Explore your costs"

### Budgets
**What:** Set custom budgets and receive alerts
**Real-life:** Like setting a monthly spending limit
**Memory Trick:** "Budgets = Budget alerts"

### Cost and Usage Report
**What:** Detailed cost and usage information
**Real-life:** Like an itemized receipt for everything
**Memory Trick:** "Usage Report = Detailed billing report"

### Pricing Calculator
**What:** Estimate costs for AWS services
**Real-life:** Like getting a quote before buying
**Memory Trick:** "Pricing Calculator = Calculate before buying"

### Savings Plans
**What:** Flexible pricing model with savings
**Two Types:**
- **Compute Savings Plans:** EC2, Lambda, Fargate (most flexible)
- **EC2 Instance Savings Plans:** Specific instance family in region
**Memory Trick:** "Savings Plans = Commit to save money"
